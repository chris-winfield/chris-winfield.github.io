This is my technical writing sample.

These documents aim to present technical software, GitHub and Atom, to a non-technical audience, creative writers. In my experience, creative writers tend to be curious, intelligent, and interested in process, but often a little scared of any technology that seems too advanced. They need a nudge to get past this. They're good at following instructions to the letter. They know more than they think they know.

The limitations of these documents are that they can't cover every use case. Readers who encounter errors won't receive many solutions here. However, the procedure is clean and correct.
