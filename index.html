Video: Jack, the Artist

Our video hopes to provide a statement on the subjective nature of art. Our protagonist is a local artist whose experimental, and, being frank, rather lackluster efforts at artistic expression haven’t garnered him any attention. He takes to the streets with a piece of kitchen decor—and is praised for it!

We hope to provide viewers an understanding that the value of art isn’t determined by popular approval, but we also want to poke fun at the artistic types who are obsessed with approval in the first place, either in an effort to gain or dismiss it.
